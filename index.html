About 'Builder's Right Hand'

This application is built to help builders or handymen in some basic measurements without a need to use any tools.
It provides you with three basic tools:
- Level
- Compass
- Ruler

Learn more

Features

Level

Level works exactly the same way as a real tool when you hold your phone in a portrait position and rotate it to left or right. The squares show you if the device is on a level surface vertically, horizontally or at a 45° angle.

Compass

Compass requires you to put your phone flat on a surface, and it will show you where the different directions are. It also shows you the direction in degrees.

Ruler

Ruler automatically adjusts to your phone and helps you measure different things. Holding your finger on a screen can show you the exact measurement and shaking your phone toggles the ruler between centimeters and inches.
